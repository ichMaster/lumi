# Лілі face pack — image prompts (v0.7, 27 portraits)

9 emotions × 3 intensity bands. Built from **one calm basis** so the character stays
identical; every other prompt is a **change‑only** delta (expression only).

**Filenames** (the v0.7 viewer / EMOTION.md §7 resolver): mid = `<emotion>.png` (the
default), low = `<emotion>_low.png`, high = `<emotion>_high.png`. `calm.png` is the basis.

## Convention — read once, applies to every prompt below

> Start from the **calm basis** image. **KEEP IDENTICAL:** her face & identity, hairstyle
> and color, outfit, background, color palette, art style, lighting, camera angle, and the
> centered head‑and‑shoulders framing (768×768), gaze toward the viewer unless noted.
> **CHANGE ONLY the facial expression** — brows, eyes, eyelids, mouth, cheeks, and a tiny
> head‑tilt where noted.

**Intensity** = how strong the expression is, nothing else:
`low` = subtle, barely‑there micro‑expression · `mid` = clear and readable (the default) ·
`high` = strong and pronounced, but natural (never a caricature).
